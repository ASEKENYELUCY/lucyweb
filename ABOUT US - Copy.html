ABOUT US

Our Lady Of Africa is a catholic founded secondary school, with both O and A level
under the strong an solid management of Mr. and Mrs. Matovu Vicent, Mr. Senyonjo Moses( Board Of Governors).
The school started in February 18 2003 with only three students and three blocks
By the end of first term, the number of students had reached to 127 and by the end of the year,
the enrollment had gone further to 365 students. This proved that OLAN was ready to the community and the country at large
With the determination and the commitment of our students and the staff, OLAN has raeched the position of being one of the giant school
in Uganda. Our teaching staff is one of the best qualified personnal with the majority holding
bachelor's degrees and masters as well. Our Lady Of Africa offers hoslitic and quality education to the nation.
