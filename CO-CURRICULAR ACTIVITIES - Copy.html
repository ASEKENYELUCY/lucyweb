EXTRA-CURRICULAR ACTIVITIES


OLAN recognizes the importance of a well-rounded education. All students are encouraged
to participate in co-curricullar activities.
These include;
1.Sports(soccer for both girls and boys, athletics,chess etc)
2.Clubs/House activities.
3. National and Regional Debates.
4. Students council.
School events include; an inter-house M.D.D competitions, Sorts Day, Fawe exhibitions,
Termly guidance and counseling, Afternoon Levers; parties etc.
